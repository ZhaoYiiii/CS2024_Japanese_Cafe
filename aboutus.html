Sunaku Cafe draws inspiration from the renowned maid cafes in Akihabara, Japan, presenting a delightful maid-concept experience. Our mission is to provide a haven of comfort for guests spanning all ages, evoking a sense of "coming home" through Japanese-inspired comfort cuisine and heartfelt hospitality. Our goal is to craft a enchanting, enjoyable, and soothing atmosphere for both our cherished guests and dedicated staff, fostering a space where everyone feels embraced and joyful!

We pride ourselves on fun and extraordinary table service, as well as providing fun entertainment including:

Magical food and drink service

Live singing and dancing performances

Monthly themed events

Commemorative polaroid decorations

To best provide the magical maid experience, Asayoru requires all guest to order at least one item!

Sunaku has an automatic 18% gratuity for all guests.

Each guest's maximum time at the cafe is 90 mins, but guests can order another item to extend their stay! During rush hour we cannot extend the time to make sure we can accommodate as many guests as possible,

We accept all major credit and debit cards! We do not accept cash unless it is exact change. Thank you for understanding!

Cafe Rules

To keep our guests and our staff members safe and provide the best experience, we kindly ask everyone to follow these simple rules!

Do not touch our maids, or ask them for their contact info! Please be respectful to all staff!

Do not treat staff as servants! Please understand that our casts are simply doing their jobs.

Do not take photos of other guests or our staff without asking! Please do take photos of food and decor!

Do not leave your seated area during performances! Please do feel free to stand up and cheer!

Do not use inappropriate languages and gestures! Please keep the space friendly for all ages!
